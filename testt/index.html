Daria is the Head of Business Development APAC Mail.Ru Group, Russia’s the largest internet company and part of the founding team of the cross-border e-commerce platform MyMALL. As the company’s first employee in China, Daria has lead the rapid growth of MyMALL in China and expanded the platform into other markets, including Korea, Thailand, Japan, and India where MyMALL is enabling its leading sellers and brand partners to market their products to the more over 100 mln customers on a number of the company’s online sales channels including Pandao, Vk.com, and Ok.Ru.

She has been living and based in China for the last 10 years and has been working in the cross-border e-commerce space since 2011. Prior to initiating Mail.Ru’s efforts in Asia, she worked for SF Express, China’s #1 express delivery company, as one of the company’s first foreign employees when it started its expansion into Russia.

A graduate from China’s Renmin University with a Masters of Economics, Daria is passionate about bringing people together, creating meaningful networks and building purposeful communities. She’s a keen supporter and member of the start-up ecosystem and community in China and globally, and she is actively involved with the organization of events including Startup Grind Shenzhen and Hitcrunch, among several others.
