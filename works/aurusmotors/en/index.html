AURUS SENAT AT FORMULA 1 RUSSIAN GRAND PRIX 2019

On 24-27 September, Sochi hosted the FORMULA 1 RUSSIAN GRAND PRIX 2019. For the sixth time one of F1 best circuits has brought the world's greatest drivers to Russia. What really stole the spotlight was the AURUS SENAT, a Russian premium car in spectacular white housed in a freestanding AURUS branded pavilion. The brand's guests, F1 partners, government delegations, representatives of the business community and show business all had an opportunity to take a look at the exterior and interior design of AURUS SENAT. Guests were provided with the best possible perspective on the races in the AURUS VIP Skybox. The introduction of a car from the AURUS family to the F1 fans of the world's most prestigious series is a landmark event in the promotion of the Russian car brand.

11.09
G-Drive Racing Has Reached the Podium with the Aurus 01 in the UK

The G-Drive Racing team, comprising Roman Rusinov, Jean-Eric Vergne and Job Van Ujtert, started the race in sixth position. The Russian team struggled during the first stage, as torrential rain soaked the track, and at one point they weren’t even in the top ten.

But then G-Drive Racing made an impressive comeback. The pilots driving the Aurus 01 prototype started moving up the field one position at a time, and by the final stage of the race they had even managed to get into first place. However, a puncture forced them to make a pit stop just five minutes before the finish, leaving the Russian team in second place at the line. The team of Rusinov, Vergne and Ujtert finished the four-hour marathon less than ten seconds behind the leader, and that despite lagging a whole lap behind early in the race.

This is the third time G-Drive Racing has reached the podium in this season of ELMS, after winning the previous two stages, and Silverstone marks the sixth time in a row that the Russian team’s drivers have taken a trophy.

With just two stages to go in the European Le-Mans Series, G-Drive Racing lead the championship with nine points between them and second place.

01.09
ANSAT HELICOPTER WITH LUXURY CABIN BY AURUS DESIGN AT MAKS 2019

At the MAKS 2019 – International aviation and space salon, Russian Helicopters Holding Company has premiered its Ansat helicopter with luxury cabin fitted in the style of the Aurus brand. The cabin was designed by Russian Helicopters technicians in cooperation with the Central Scientific Research Automobile and Automotive Engines Institute.

The design process for the cabin involved the use of computer modeling as well as testing with people of various heights and sizes to develop optimal ergonomic solutions. The Ansat helicopter features the biggest cabin in its class, which combined with cutting-edge design solutions, translates into more space for passengers. The helicopter also features a sensor-controlled window dimming system employing technology used here for the first time in the VIP cabin of a helicopter. Extra comfort is provided via personal lighting and air vents for each passenger, and in addition there are sockets for charging gadgets.

31.07
G-Drive Racing Team in Aurus 01 Wins ELMS Round in Barcelona

Russia’s G-Drive Racing, competing in endurance racing in the Aurus 01 race car, came first in the third round of the European Le Mans Series held in July in Barcelona (Spain).

This triumph in the “4 Hours of Barcelona” marathon is the team’s second victory in a row in this ELMS season and twenty-fifth in total in international endurance racing.

Roman Rusinov, driver and manager of G-Drive Racing: “Overall, the race was ideal. Our Aurus 01 prototype was fast and reliable, my co-drivers gave excellent performance in their stints, we pitted in good time benefiting from the yellow flag phases, and everyone in the team did an excellent job. We demonstrated yet again that G-Drive Racing is the strongest team!”

18.02
Aurus enlisted a new strategic investor

Abu Dhabi, February 18. - TAWAZUN Defence and Security Development Fund, FSUE "NAMI" and SOLLERS PJSC signed the Agreement on TAWAZUN Fund Joining the AURUS LLC Participants. The Agreement has been signed within the ongoing IDEX exhibition in the presence of Denis Manturov, the Minister of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation, Abdullah Al Nahyan, the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the United Arab Emirates, and Khaldoon Khalifa Al Mubarak, Managing Director and Group Chief Executive Officer of the Mubadala Investment Company.

"According to the signed Agreement, the TAWAZUN Fund will obtain a 36% stake in AURUS LLC and will invest a total of 110 mln euro into the company within the next three years.  These financial resources will be invested to arrange the AURUS vehicles full-scale production, their localisation, and also for furtherance of distribution in the worldwide markets. The Agreement also establishes that the TAWAZUN Fund will become the master distributor of the AURUS vehicle line-up in the MENA region. The TAWAZUN Fund entry and joining the AURUS project is a telling evidence of the fact that the luxury vehicle line-up developed in Russia has every chance to become a successful product in the global market and that the project is commercially effective and attractive to potential investors", stated Head of Minpromtorg of Russia, Denis Manturov.

FSUE "NAMI" will continue to be the developer of the AURUS-branded vehicles and will keep the 63.5% share in the company.   The SOLLERS PJSC share in AURUS LLC will be reduced to 0.5%, and upon that, beginning from the end of 2020, SOLLERS will become the Project industrial partner and will launch the series production of the AURUS vehicles at the automobile plant in Alabuga Special Economic Zone (Alabuga SEZ).

TAWAZUN Chief Executive Officer Tareq Abdul Raheem Al Hosani underlined, "We appreciate the strategic partnership with NAMI within the AURUS Project and are encouraged by the forthcoming cooperation on the deployment of production of the luxury and armoured vehicles for the Russian and international markets."

"It is obvious that the agreement which was signed today between the TAWAZUN Fund and AURUS LLC reflects the full marks and appreciation of the AURUS vehicles for the international investments. We are glad that the R&D achievements of the Russian engineers in the automotive field have received such a great recognition", noted Gerhard Hilgert, Chief Executive Officer of AURUS LLC.

The World Premiere of the luxury Russian vehicles AURUS SENAT and AURUS SENAT Limousine

August 29, 2018 – the World Premiere of the luxury Russian vehicles AURUS SENAT and AURUS SENAT Limousine took place within the Moscow International Automobile Salon.

For the first time in the modern Russia, the AURUS vehicles development gave birth to uncompromising level of ballistic and explosion protection implemented significantly exceeding the similar parameters of the vehicles of competing brands. The armored AURUS SENAT Limousine is an ocular demonstration of the Russian engineers' achievements in this segment. The AURUS SENAT belongs to the commercial unarmored series.

Denis Manturov, the Minister of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation emphasized the importance of the event for the domestic automotive industry development, "For the first time in the Russian history, a project aimed to release a commercial line-up of luxury vehicles has emerged. AURUS is a national brand, which represents modern, technically developed and innovative Russia. I am convinced that AURUS will have a success and that the AURUS vehicles will soon take over their niche both at the domestic and export markets."

Franz Gerhard Hilgert, CEO of AURUS LLC, said, "AURUS offers a highly competitive product with a possibility to customize the vehicle through personal contact of the client with the designer and engineer, which will be an important advantage when buying the vehicle. Future owners of the AURUS vehicles are offered freedom in choosing the vehicle exterior and interior solutions. At present, we are making preparations for the Russian and international start of sales and working on attraction of new partners to organize the distribution and aftersales processes. The European premiere of the AURUS vehicles will take place at the Geneva International Motor Show in 2019."

The AURUS SENAT and AURUS SENAT Limousine offer a combination of the all-wheel drive and hybrid unit The vehicles were developed specially for the Russian conditions taking into account climate and road peculiarities of the country, which ensures the maximum level of comfort and safety under any operating conditions. The vehicles are equipped with a high-performance V8 engine, which is a new in-house development created especially for AURUS vehicles.
